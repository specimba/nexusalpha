ROTATION STRATEGY — TASK-BASED MODEL SWITCHING
================================================================================

ARCHITECTURE/PLANNING SESSION:
  pi --model "NIM-Kimi-K2.5"
  WHY: Best reasoning + reliable tool use at reasonable speed.
       Thinks deeply before acting. Handles complex multi-file refactors.

CODING/IMPLEMENTATION SESSION:
  pi --model "NIM-Qwen3-Coder"
  WHY: Purpose-built for code. 1 second response. Handles tests, edits, git.

QUICK FIX / SMALL TASK:
  pi --model "NIM-Step-Flash"
  WHY: 600ms response. For "just fix this typo" or "add this import".

DEEP RESEARCH / COMPLEX DESIGN:
  pi --model "NIM-GLM5"
  WHY: Highest quality thinking on NIM. Use when you need the best answer,
       not the fastest answer.

INSIDE PI — SWITCH ON THE FLY:
  Ctrl+L → pick model from list
  Ctrl+P → cycle to next model
  Shift+Ctrl+P → cycle to previous model
